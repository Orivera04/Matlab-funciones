If you are an instructor and wish to request solutions to the exercises in the book, please email: solutions@cambridge.org. To request a password for the CUP solutions set, please email solutions@cambridge.org and state the following information:

  • The title, author and ISBN of the textbook: Solving ODE's with Matlab, Shampine, Gladwell, Thompson, 0521530946
  • Your name and academic postal address
  • The name of the course you will be using the solutions for and the URL for the course (if any)
  • The level of the course
  • The number of students expected to enroll
  • The start date of the course
  • The contact information of your campus/local bookstore
