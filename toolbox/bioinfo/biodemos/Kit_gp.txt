            Summary: KIT encodes the human homolog of the proto-oncogene c-kit.
            C-kit was first identified as the cellular homolog of the feline   
            sarcoma viral oncogene v-kit.  KIT is a type 3 transmembrane       
            receptor  for MGF (mast cell growth factor, also known as stem cell
            factor).  Mutations in KIT are associated with gastrointestinal    
            stromal tumors, mast cell disease, acute myelogenous lukemia, and  
            piebaldism.
